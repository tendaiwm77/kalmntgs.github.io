About Us

Your vision, our expertise. Creating tomorrow, today.

Introduction

Thank you for considering KALM.Ntg Architects (Pty) Ltd for your professional service needs. We offer flexible, tailored solutions.

The Company

KALM.Ntg Architects (Pty) Ltd is a multidisciplinary practice composed of professionals in Architecture, Civil & Structural Engineering, Quantity Surveying, and Project Management.

Vision

Good design is central to improving the quality of life in homes, workplaces, and communities. We strive to create sustainable, impactful spaces and aim to lead as a 100% black-owned firm committed to B-BBEE principles and inclusive growth.

Mission Statement

We are committed to design innovation, environmental care, and sound project management. We value excellence in the built environment.

Company Background

Established in 2012, KALM.Ntg Architects (Pty) Ltd is a South African 100% B-BBEE black-owned practice. Founded by passionate professionals, we deliver excellent results tailored to our clients' needs.
